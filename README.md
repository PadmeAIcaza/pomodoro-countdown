The Pomodoro technique is a time management method in which you do focused work during 25-minute intervals, small breaks in-between of 5 minutes, and a rewarding 20-minute breaks

# Functionality
## Work
- Lasts 25 minutes
- Once timer is over, adds a checkmark and switches to Break (pink)
## Break (pink)
- Lasts 5 minutes
- Once timer is over, switches to Work (again)
## Break (red)
- Happens once we reached a total of 4 checkmarks
- Lasts 20 minutes

### Other notes
- Timer does not stop on its own
- It can get reach an unlimited amount of checkmarks
- User can restart timer
